About Us

Who Are We

MOHAMMED JAFFER SHERIFF is the driving force and inspiration behind TAWAKKAL VENTURES. To him it's all about providing unique Design Detailing in to Construction practice that exceed the expectations of his customers at the same time keeping up with the international trends within all Three business verticals. . All his works are done with passion, purpose, sensitivity and humility, thus strive to create unique, inspirational, and path breaking structures and landmarks that stand the test of time.

Tawakkal Ventures is a construction company focused on Residential and Commercial properties which also provides all types of civil and allied services. We are present in 20+ locations in Bangalore and are on track to expand more, one of our latest projects boasts on building institutions and commercial complexs at prime locations.

Tawakkal group of companies was founded in the year 1983 by Late Mr. Abdul Subhan an entrepreneur and a philanthropist who started off as an industrialist and slowly gave the business a shape of a company. Over the last few decades Tawakkal ventures a branch of Tawakkal group made its name in Bangalore for rendering service in an impeccable manner which has always left its clients with what they wanted.

Tawakkal Ventures is led by its founding partner Mohammed Jaffer Sheriff who has diverse knowledge in the field of building and construction, whose mission is to provide homes that make our clients have a convenient lifestyle. Tawakkal Ventures has its own team who are experts in the field of construction/development and provides its products/services after it goes through niche research and due diligence carried out by professionals.
